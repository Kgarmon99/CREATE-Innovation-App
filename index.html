About This Event

The Launch Kentucky Idea Challenge is open to innovators from 13 counties in South Central Kentucky plus WKU students. Submit your innovative business idea for a chance to win $10,000 in funding and gain access to mentorship from the CREATE network.

Eligibility:

  • Must reside in one of 13 eligible counties OR be a current WKU student
  • Early-stage ideas (pre-revenue or early revenue)
  • Solo founders or teams up to 4 people

Judging Criteria:

  • Innovation and creativity (30%)
  • Market opportunity (25%)
  • Feasibility (25%)
  • Team capability (20%)
